About Me个人简介

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Sociology at the Center for Social Research, Peking University, supervised by Professors Jia Yu and Yu Xie. I will join Princeton University as a Peking-Princeton Postdoctoral Research Associate in 2026–2028. Before that, I received my bachelor’s degree in Demography from Renmin University of China.

I am broadly interested in family change, social stratification, and social attitudes in contemporary China and the United States. My research examines how family transitions unfold across social groups and across the life course, and how family norms and behaviors co-evolve over time. Methodologically, my work draws on family demography and historical text analysis to study family transitions.

我是北京大学社会研究中心社会学博士候选人，导师为於嘉长聘副教授和谢宇教授。2026 至 2028 年间，我将担任北京大学—普林斯顿大学联合博士后研究员。在此之前，我于中国人民大学获得人口学方向学士学位。

我主要关注当代中国与美国的家庭变迁、社会分层和社会态度。我的研究主要考察家庭形成的生命历程如何在不同社会群体中展开，以及家庭规范与家庭行为如何随时间共同演变。在方法上，我目前主要运用定量社会学、人口学方法和历史文本分析分析家庭变迁。
